Eddie Zhang

About

Eddie Zhang is a fourth-year medical student at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai in New York, NY. He is the Co-Founder and Chief Operating Officer of Progressive Health Partnership, a 501(c)3 organization that works on issues of maternal and child health in Southwestern Uganda. In addition to being an aspiring oncologist and global health advocate, he is passionate about issues of mortality and end-of-life care, harnessing the power of technology for social good, and lifehacking. In his spare time, he brews beer in his tiny New York City apartment. Eddie received a B.A. in English Literature from Duke University in 2010.
