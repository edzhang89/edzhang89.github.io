Hi, I'm Eddie Zhang.

I'm a resident physician in Radiation Oncology at Fox Chase Cancer Center in Philadelphia, PA. I'm the Co-Founder and Chief Operating Officer of Progressive Health Partnership, a 501(c)3 organization that works on issues of maternal and child health in Southwestern Uganda. I'm passionate about issues of mortality and end-of-life care, harnessing the power of technology for social good, and lifehacking. In my spare time, I brew beer in my tiny Philadelphia apartment. I asked nicely for a B.A. in English Literature from Duke University in 2010, and I successfully groveled for a M.D. from Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai in 2015. I completed my internship training in Internal Medicine at Abington Jefferson Health.
